Introduction

Wen-Di Shen (Student Member, IEEE) received the B.S. degree in electrical engineering in 2016 from National Taiwan University, Taipei, Taiwan, where he is currently working toward the Ph.D. degree in communication engineering with the Graduate Institute of Communication Engineering.

Since 2016, he has been with Wireless Mobile Networking Laboratory led by Prof. H.Y. Wei. His research interests includes UAV communications, 3GPP standards, V2X communications, and fifth-generation technologies.
